Using the scheduler

Interview teams

The first step is to create your interview teams. From the college list page, click on the "Teams" link for your College under the "Interviews" column. This will present you with a fillable form. Detais you have already entered for your teams will fill the first forms.

To add a team, fill out at least the "Names" field in the last (blank) form. Location and notes are optional; if you use the database to make a printable version of your schedule, these will be printed as well, but otherwise the databae doesn't use this information. Then press Update button below the form.

To edit a team's information, simply edit the information in place and press the Update button.

To delete a team, tick the Delete checkbox below the form for the team and press the Update button.

You can edit, delete, and add teams all with the same update.

If you click on another College's Teams link, you should see the teams they entered, but simply as an uneditable list.

Interview schedule

The interview schedule page is accessed by clicking on the "Schedule" link for your College under the "Interviews" column. This page lists candidates and their interview slots for each interview team. It also lists interviews which have been scheduled at other Colleges for these candidates. Interviews which fall within 20 minutes of other interviews should be flagged as a potential conflict (the logic is not perfect, however, but at least one of the conflicting interviews should be flagged in most circumstances).

The interview table can be sorted according to the columns by clicking in the column header.

To view interviews scheduled for an individual candidate, click on the candidate's name in the left column.

To view interviews scheduled for a team, click on the team's name in the table header.

To edit the interview schedule for a team, click on the "Edit" link next to the team's name in the table header.

Editing interview slots

The easiest way to get started is to click on the Physics interviews or Philosophy interviews button. These buttons select the relevant candidates and, if no interviews have been scheduled for them yet, fills in some default values for the interview type and subject.

The interview type options are local, remote, or no interview. Use "local" for face-to-face interviews, and "remote" for skype interviews.

The subject options are physics or philosophy. "Physics", in this case, also includes mathematics, i.e., the normal interview subjects for normal physics candidates.

You can fill out as few or as many interview slots as you'd like at any given time. Previous slots will be filled into the form, ready for editing.

If you want to leave the schedule unchanged, just leave the page without saving interview slots.

To save the interview slots, click on the Save all slots button. Note that all complete interview slots are saved (or re-saved, as the case may be), whether or not they have been selected using the checkboxes on the left side.

Red is used to indicate where there may not be enough time between interviews. It is currently assumed that 20 minutes is the minimum time between interviews for any individual candidate. However, the system won't prevent you from saving or printing out schedules with conflicts.
